motivation

Each year, I speak with both undergraduate and Master’s students in Sociology at Saint Louis University on presenting research. Presenting research, whether it is a capstone project, a Master’s thesis, or a guest lecture, can be daunting for students. My goal is to help students develop skills for high quality, effective presentations. This site catalogues material related to both my undergraduate and graduate ‘presentations on presentations’ and provides resources for students on presentation design.
